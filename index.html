A TRUE LOVE STORY

TO MY LOVING BABY PRINCESS

For the Love of My Life

By Mohammad Ameerah

Page 1: The Warmth of You

Loving you feels like waking up to the soft touch of sunlight through the window gentle, golden, and full of promise. From the first moment you entered my life, something in me changed. You became the first sip of coffee in the morning, the comforting warmth that fills the soul and reminds me that everything is going to be okay. You calm my chaos. You complete my mornings. You make life make sense; you make everything we do special, simple talk on the side walk can create memories for us to remember all our life

Page 2: You See Me

With you, I feel seen not just looked at, but understood. You read me in ways I can’t explain. I don’t have to hide, fake, or pretend. You take me as I am, flaws and all, and love me anyway. That kind of love It’s rare. It’s real. It’s you. You’re my safe space, my steady ground, the person who knows my silence and listens to it, the person I can show my inner child to.

Page 3: A Year and a Half of Magic

We’ve been together for a year and a half now, but every moment still feels brand new. You turn the ordinary into magic, you are heaven on earth. A text, walk, a lazy afternoon, a simple hug everything is brighter with you. We’ve built a rhythm, a connection that feels timeless, like we’ve known each other in every life before this. You’re not just part of my life you’re the best part, and you are not jst a part , you are my whole life, the reason I live

Page 4: Everything You Are

You’re beautiful, absolutely beautiful but that’s just the beginning. Your beauty stops time. When the sun hits your face and your brown eyes shine like molten gold, I swear I could lose myself in them forever, one look from you can control me. Your lips make me want to pause the world, just to kiss you, to feel that closeness that no words could ever replace. And your smile, that smile changed everything. It’s what made me fall in love with you the first time we met. And still, to this day, every time I see that smile or hear your laughter, I fall even harder. There’s magic in the way you carry yourself, the way you walk the way you sit, the way you talk, the way you smell, the way you eat, grace in every movement, and warmth in every glance. But it’s not just how you look, it’s how you are. You’re thoughtful, driven, kind, respectful, cute. You chase your goals. You show strength wrapped in softness. Being with you has inspired me to be more, to do more, to live with purpose. You don’t just make me feel loved you make me want to be the best version of myself.

Page 5: My Best Friend

You’re the first person I want to talk to when I wake up in the morning and the last person I want to talk to before I sleep, the one I think of when something great happens, when I hear some news or stories, or when I need comfort. Your laughter is my favorite sound. Your voice calms every storm. You’re my best friend. My partner. My home, the mother of my children, my everything. Whether we’re out or in, talking or just sitting in silence it’s perfect because it’s with you, your existence alone makes my soul rest easy, the fact that you live life with me makes my heart skip a beat every time and draw a smile on my face, you are all I could ask for, you are perfect, words can’t describe how perfect you are.

Page 6: Our Future

When I close my eyes and picture the future, you’re in it always. Laughing in the kitchen. Holding my hand through hard times. Cheering me on. Dancing in the living room to our favorite song. I see a life full of quiet moments, loud joy, and unshakable love. A life where we grow older, never apart. A life where we choose each other—every day, all I live for is a future with you, you make me want to skip time and make the years go by faster just so I can live life with you, wake up every day beside you and look into your eyes knowing I fulfilled every promise I gave you, working harder every day so I can make you live a life you’ve always dreamed of, making sure nothing is on your mind and that everything you ask for is available for you whenever you want.

Page 7: How You Changed Me

Before you, I didn’t know how soft love could be, I didn’t know love. I didn’t know how deeply I could care for someone, you sked me one day while holding my hand and looking at me with those gorgeous eyes if I imagined I would ever love someone like this, no one in this whole world could ever have felt half of the love I gave you, you made me like this, I changed for changed for you and only you. You made me better. You made me gentler. You helped me see beauty in places I used to overlook. You brought light to my darkest corners. You showed me that love can be peaceful, and safe, and still full of passion.

Page 8: We’re Unbreakable

Even when life throws its worst, I don’t worry—because I have you. We don’t have to be perfect to be right. We just have to keep showing up. And we do. Every time. You’re not just the love of my life—you’re my teammate, my anchor, my rock. With you, I feel strong enough to face anything, nothing in this world could ever tear us apart, me and you are forever, no person or fight or anything could ever make me step aside, I’ll fight the world for you, protect you from every single thing that could come Infront of you my baby.

Page 9: Your Love in Action

You love me in the little things—your quiet presence, your thoughtful words, the way your eyes hold mine like I’m the only person in the world. But it’s your touch that unravels me every time. Your hands on mine, the warmth of your arms around me, the softness of your fingers brushing my hair—these are the moments that soothe me. Your touch calms every storm, no matter how loud it is in my head. And no matter how many times I feel it, I never get used to it. It always feels new. Special. Like the first time. Like it was made just for me. And I want you to know—I will love you back in every way your heart understands. In physical touch, I’ll never let you forget how wanted you are. I’ll hold you when you’re tired, kiss your forehead when you’re stressed, run my hands through your hands and knee for now hehe just because you like the way it feels. I’ll wrap you in the kind of hugs that say, “You’re safe here. Always.” In words of affirmation, I’ll remind you every day that you are beautiful, that you are loved, that I am endlessly proud to be yours. I’ll write you notes, send you messages, whisper sweet truths into your ear—not because I have to, but because my heart needs to tell you these things. In quality time, you’ll have my full attention. No distractions. No half-listening. Just me and you—talking, laughing, doing nothing or everything. I’ll take you on long walks, spontaneous road trips, movie nights, or just sit with you in quiet comfort. Time with you is never wasted—it’s the best part of my day. Through acts of service, I’ll show you love in the things I do—big or small. Whether it’s helping you when you're overwhelmed, running errands so you don’t have to, or making your favorite meal just because you had a hard day, I’ll do it all with love, with the quiet intention of making your life lighter. Your happiness will always matter to me. And in gifts, I’ll never aim for extravagance—I’ll aim for meaning. A flower on a random day, a letter on a rainy day, a playlist full of songs that remind me of you(coming soon hehe). I want to give you things that speak to your heart, not your hands. Every gift will be a reflection of how well I know you—and how deeply I love you. Above all, I’ll give you respect. The kind of respect that honors your voice, your dreams, your boundaries. I’ll never speak over you. I’ll never make you feel small. I’ll always lift you, support you, and see you for who you truly are. You deserve to be cherished in every language love speaks—and I’m going to spend the rest of my life doing just that.

Page 10: Forever, One Sip at a Time

A year and a half has passed, and my heart is still overflowing. You are still the first sip of coffee in the morning—the one thing I look forward to, the comfort I crave, the spark that starts my day. You are my light, my love, my everything. And if this is just the beginning… I can’t wait for forever.

Final Dedication

Forever yours, always your love — Mohammad
